1. About the App

Aumo is a voice-first translation application designed for short, natural conversations.

The App allows users to:

  • translate recorded speech;
  • translate typed text;
  • translate between Thai, Russian, Spanish, and English;
  • select translation direction using Their Voice and My Voice;
  • adjust voice, tone, and certain language-specific settings;
  • request a new translation of the same source text;
  • save successful translations in on-device History;
  • copy translated text to the system clipboard;
  • submit optional categorical feedback about a translation; and
  • switch between system, light, and dark appearance modes.

Aumo does not just translate words. It attempts to translate how you want to sound. However, the controls provided by the App do not guarantee that every translation will reflect the exact intended meaning, tone, gender, voice, level of formality, dialect, or regional usage.
